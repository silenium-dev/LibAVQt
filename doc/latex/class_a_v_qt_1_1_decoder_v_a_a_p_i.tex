\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
VAAPI accelerated video decoder. 

Decodes video from QIODevice into single frames, that are passed to every via register\+Callback registered callback. It does {\itshape {\bfseries{not}}} stop when no callbacks are registered, so make sure, that either at least one callback is registered or the decoder is paused, or frames will be dropped
